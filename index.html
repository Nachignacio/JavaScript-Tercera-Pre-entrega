About Me

You probably don't know who I am so:
My name is Ignacio Manfredini. I'm an Argentinian musician amateur wanna-be and I play piano since I have use of memory. This is a virtual space I made to myself to keep progress of what I can improve with my piano skills and motivate myself to keep practicing and compose appealing rearranges of songs people would love to hear with a more classical and melodic touch. That said, I'd eventually like to use this page as portfolio in the future. I'm 28 years old and I may not play as good as someone that has been practicing long hours everyday of his life, but I like to polish my listening skills trying to play every song I can by ear. In the long run, I would like to end up as skillfull as many pianists I admire that have a well-deserved reputation over social media.That's why since some years ago I started opening up request spaces in my social media to let people know that I'm willing to rearrange songs they'd like to hear on piano. Over time, this grew up and my content accumulated more than I had anticipated.
So this is it, the conclusion of why I made this page: to keep a tidy space to organice my uploads and make a more official channel to keep track of requests of songs that people would like me to play or rearrange.
Thank you for reading!
